Mentor Guidelines

Overview

An industry mentor is defined as a professional who volunteers their time, experience, and guidance to support students as they navigate academic, professional, and personal development within their field of interest. Mentors act as role models, sounding boards, and connectors—providing students with valuable exposure to career pathways, industry expectations, and practical advice.

‌The Geekulcha Student Society (GKSS) is aimed at getting students into Tech and Innovation Leadership. to help students on campus in generating an understanding of the digital environment, for them to become inspired, and enabling life-changing innovation. ‌Geekulcha values growing the GKSS Leaders who in turn, help the growth of students on campus on various aspects of career development.


Your Role as A GKSS Mentor

‌A GKSS Mentor is an experienced professional from the industry who provides guidance, support, expertise, motivation and inspiration to help the GKSS society push forward their vision in a specific focus area. The responsibilities of a GKSS Mentor include but are not limited to the following:

  • To share insights on emerging technologies, industry best practices, and real world applications
  • To organize mentorship sessions for the society and participate in society panel discussions
  • To offer guidance on navigating challenges in their tech careers and society initiatives
  • Provide advice on career paths, internships, and industry expectations.

    • It is to note that the role of you being a mentor applies to the GKSS leaders that govern and run the societies at the individual educational institutions, they in turn will use any insights and guidance garnered from you to then push forward tech innovation and leadership to their respective student bodies and/or surrounding communities pushing forward the initiative of Training the Trainer.


    Why Be An Industry Mentor

    Mentorship is a two-way relationship with many benefits:

    For You (Mentor)
    • Give back to the community and help shape future talent.
    • Stay connected with emerging trends and fresh perspectives.
    • Expand your professional network within the university ecosystem.
    • Fulfill personal and professional development goals, including leadership and communication skills.

    For The Student (Mentee)
    • Gain real-world insights and career guidance.
    • Build confidence through professional engagement.
    • Learn about industry norms, tools, and opportunities.
    • Receive constructive feedback on projects and aspirations.


    On Key Responsibilities and Time Commitments

    As an Industry Mentor, your primary role is to foster professional growth, inspire confidence, and provide practical guidance to the GKSS Leaders, While every mentor-mentee relationship is unique, some core responsibilities include:

    Relationship Building
    • Establish a foundation of trust and open communication.
    • Set clear expectations and boundaries early in the mentorship.
    • Develop a genuine interest in your mentee’s journey to drive tech innovation forward.
    Career and Academic Guidance
    • Share your career path and industry experiences—both successes and challenges.
    • Provide insights into workplace culture, expectations, and emerging trends in your field.
    • Help mentees explore and clarify their chapter interests, goals, and options.
    • Encourage the development of transferable skills such as communication, teamwork, and time management.
    Ongoing Engagement
    • Maintain regular communication with your mentee, whether via email, video calls, or in-person meetings.
    • Be a reliable sounding board for questions, concerns, and ideas.
    • Provide honest but supportive feedback, and help mentees reflect on their progress.
    Networking and Exposure
    • Introduce mentees to other professionals or relevant communities (where appropriate).
    • Recommend resources, industry events, webinars, or learning opportunities.
    • Join mentorship mixers, panels, or roundtable discussions hosted by the student society to share your perspective.

    Support For Mentors

    We understand that effective mentorship requires the right support and structure, especially for busy professionals. That’s why Geekulcha is committed to providing a well-organized and rewarding experience for our mentors. As a mentor, you’ll receive exclusive access to our dedicated GKSS Mentor's Platform, designed to streamline your involvement and enhance the mentorship experience.
    Through this platform, you can:

  • Connect directly with your assigned chapters (as well as other chapters) via built-in messaging system.
  • Track all the chapters' growth with insights to analytics
  • Join a GKSS community forum to share insights, ask questions, and connect with other fellow mentors.
  • Provide advice on career paths, internships, and industry expectations.

    • This platform makes it easy to stay organized, save time, and focus on what really matters: supporting the GKSS leaders meaningfully. By creating a mentorship experience that is structured, supported, and rewarding, we hope to empower mentors to make a lasting difference without placing unnecessary demands on their time.
